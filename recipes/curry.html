Vegan Thai Curry

Description

This Vegan Thai Curry recipe is spicy, delicious and healthy!

It's the perfect, satisfying veggie-packed meal that delivers comfort in every bite.

Ingridients:

  • Curry paste
  • Chilli peppers
  • Ginger
  • Onion, Garlic, Carrot
  • Cauliflower
  • Other Veggies of choice
  • Virgin Olive Oil
  • Coconut Milk
  • Spices: salt, pepper, curcuma, curry
  • Rice
  • Coriander

Steps:

  1. Cook the aromatics
  2. Add Veggies
  3. Finish with coconout milk
  4. Serve with fresh herbs and rice
