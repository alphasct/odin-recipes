Holly Guacamolly

Description

Delicious and healthy blend of avocado and fresh limes juice

Simple dip of Mexican origin

Ingridients:

  • Ripe Avos
  • Chilli peppers
  • Limes
  • Garlic
  • Fresh coriander
  • Virgin Olive Oil
  • Salt n Pepper
  • Cumin

Steps:

  1. Squeeze Avos with a fork
  2. Add lime juice, spices
  3. Mix with chopped garlic
  4. Little bit of Olive Oil please
  5. Blend
  6. Delicately infuse coriander
