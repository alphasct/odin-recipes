Creamy Roasted Red Pepper Soup

Spicy, nice and easy!

Ingridients:

  • Roasted red peppers
  • Chilli peppers
  • Pelatti Tomatoes
  • Onion, Garlic, Carrot
  • Water
  • Virgin Olive Oil
  • Unsweetened plant cream
  • Spices: salt, pepper, curcuma
  • Good quality vegatable stock
  • Fresh herbs

Steps:

  1. Cook the aromatics
  2. Build & simmer
  3. Blend the soup
  4. Finish with cream
  5. Serve with fresh herbs
