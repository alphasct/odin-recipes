Carrot & Ginger Soup

Description

Beautiful and healthy dish to warm you up

Source of vitamins, health and beauty...

Ingridients:

  • Carrot
  • Ginger
  • Chili peppers
  • Onion, Garlic, Carrot
  • Water
  • Virgin Olive Oil
  • Unsweetened coconout milk
  • Spices: salt, pepper, curcuma
  • Good quality vegatable stock
  • Fresh herbs
  • Couscous

Steps:

  1. Cook pan carrots
  2. Mix with spices
  3. Blend with water
  4. Finish with cream
  5. Serve with fresh herbs and couscous
